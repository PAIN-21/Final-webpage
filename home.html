Anime

Anime is a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It covers a wide range of genres, including action, romance, fantasy, and science fiction. Many anime series and films have gained cult followings and have had a significant impact on popular culture.

From classics like "Naruto" and "Dragon Ball Z" to modern hits like "Attack on Titan" and "My Hero Academia," anime continues to captivate audiences with its compelling storytelling, vibrant artwork, and memorable characters.
